Part 1

I had a best friend who would often talk badly about me to my friends. We had an imaginary world on a piece of paper where we had countries, and I was sort of part of a party, but it was just my best friend and me. We had secret weapons there and other secret stuff. The conflict started after he and my other friend had their "very" strong weapon, and they were flexing it to everyone. They told each other the secret weapon they had, but not me, and I asked them to tell me, but my best friend told him not to, that it's just their secret. I was not happy about that, so I went home and went to sleep. When I woke up and went to school, all my friends stopped hanging out and were distant. When I asked them why they'd just ignore me. When I got home, I got more calls from my former friends saying they don't want to be friends with me. After a while of arguing with them, I hung up and accepted it. After a few days, I made a friend, he was very chill and didn't care, just because of a random made-up game. Later, I found out from a neutral friend why they kicked me out of their friend group: it was because I had leaked their secret weapons. Which was completely made up, but I didn't care because their true colors were shown. Thank you for reading part 2, coming out later.
